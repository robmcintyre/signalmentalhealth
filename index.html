Welcome to Signal Mental Health dot Org
 
 
The purpose of this group is to provide support for those who have or are currently strugging with their mental health.
 
Requirements for Membership
 
1. Must be a friend or family or fellow patient.
2. Must be respectful to all member of the group
3. Must show compassion to all other members of the group
4. Must provide support when other members are in crisis, if able to.
5. No negative comments regarding others in the group. If there is a problem with the behaviour of another member, grievances must be submitted publicly and with respect and compassion.
6. Membership is voluntary. Members may leave the group at any time
7. Members cannot be removed unless they have broken a rule above
